What makes a good passphrase?

  • Long enough to be hard to guess
  • Not a famous quotation from literature, holy books, et cetera
  • Hard to guess by intuition--even by someone who knows the user well
  • Easy to remember and type accurately
  • For better security, any easily memorable encoding at your own level can be applied.
  • Not reused between sites, applications and other different sources.

Remeber to make backups

Your passwords are stored locally on your browser so please make use of the export function to make regular backups. The exported file is encrypted using your passphrase so what ever you do don't forget it.
